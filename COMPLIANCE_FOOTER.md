# Email Compliance Footer

**Use this footer on ALL marketing emails to comply with FTC and CAN-SPAM requirements.**

---

## Standard Footer (copy-paste at end of every email)

```
---

**IMPORTANT DISCLOSURES:**

Results and earnings are not guaranteed. Results vary based on individual effort, experience, and market conditions. Income claims represent past performance, which is not a guarantee of future results. Your results may differ materially.

**CAN-SPAM COMPLIANCE:**

PRINTMAXX
ATTN: Compliance
[Your Physical Business Address]
[City, State ZIP]

You're receiving this because you opted in to the PRINTMAXX email list.

[Unsubscribe Link]
[Manage Preferences Link]

This communication is from an automated marketing service and may include affiliate links.
```

---

## For Income/Earnings Claims

Add this disclaimer NEAR the claim (not just at footer):

```
*Results may vary. Your actual results depend on effort, market conditions, and other factors. See disclosures below.*
```

---

## For Affiliate Links

Add disclosure:

```
[Affiliate Link Disclosure: This link includes my affiliate code. I may earn a commission if you purchase, at no extra cost to you.]
```

---

## Compliance Checklist

- [ ] All income/earnings claims have "Results may vary" disclaimer
- [ ] Physical mailing address included in footer
- [ ] Unsubscribe link present and functional
- [ ] CAN-SPAM compliance statement included
- [ ] Affiliate disclosures on all affiliate links
- [ ] No misleading subject lines

---

**Note:** This footer must be added to ALL emails in:
- EMAIL/sequences/
- EMAIL/ecom_outreach/
- EMAIL/campaigns/
- Any marketing emails generated by PRINTMAXX

Without this footer, emails may violate FTC and CAN-SPAM regulations.
